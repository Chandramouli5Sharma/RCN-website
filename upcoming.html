Line Follower Workshop

A hands-on workshop to build and program your first autonomous robot.

Dive into the world of robotics with our Line Follower Workshop! This event is designed for enthusiasts of all skill levels, from complete beginners to those looking to refine their skills. You'll get to build, program, and test your very own autonomous robot that can intelligently follow a black line on a white surface.

What You'll Learn

  • Robot Assembly: Get hands-on experience assembling a robot chassis, mounting motors, and connecting all the components.
  • Sensor Integration: Understand how IR sensors work to detect lines and also build a custom IR array for your bot.
  • Microcontroller Programming: Learn the basics of programming an Arduino or similar microcontroller to read sensor data and control motor movements.

Why Participate?

  • Build a complete, functional robot from scratch.
  • Gain practical skills in electronics, programming, and mechanical design.
  • Add a cool, hands-on project to your portfolio.
  • Collaborate with fellow robotics enthusiasts and learn from experienced mentors.
